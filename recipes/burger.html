Smashed beef burgers

The smashed burger is a great dish for family and friends

Smashing the patty gives it more surface area resulting in delicious grilled crust

Ingredients needed

  • brioche buns
  • Lettuce
  • tomato
  • minced beef(chuck)
  • conditments
  • Bacon

Instructions

  1. Shape the minced beef iinto patties
  2. grill them. after a minute, flatten them as best as you can with a brick
  3. Slice the buns into 2. Add sliced tomatoes and lettuce
  4. add your preffered sauces and place the freshly grilled patty on top
